\textbf{Question:} \\

Find the value of $k$ for which the matrix is singular.
\begin{align*}
\myvec{k & 8 \\ 1 & 2k}
\end{align*}

\textbf{Solution:}\\

Singular matrix:
\begin{align}
\det\myvec{k & 8 \\ 1 & 2k} &= 0 \\
k \cdot 2k - 1 \cdot 8 &= 0\\
2k^2 - 8 &= 0 \\
2k^2 &= 8 \\
k^2 &= 4 
\end{align}
\begin{align}
\boxed{
k = \pm 2}
\end{align}
